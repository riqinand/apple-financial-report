## Company Overview
Apple Inc. designs, manufactures, and markets smartphones, personal computers, tablets, wearables, and accessories worldwide. It operates through Products and Services segments, offering iPhone, Mac, iPad, Apple Watch, Apple TV, AirPods, HomePod, and various digital services such as iCloud, Apple Music, and Apple Pay.
